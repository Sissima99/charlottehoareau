Beazy UG

December 2020, January 2021 & February 2021 by Charlotte

I did a 3-month internship as a Social Media Marketing Specialist for Beazy UG. Beazy is a platform where European photographers and filmmakers (mostly based in Berlin) can share their gear with each other. You can either rent your own gear when you’re not using it, or find anything you might need from cameras, lenses, drones and even studio or photoshooting spaces! The start-up hosts a lot of photography events including photowalks, workshops, exhibitions and meetups bringing together photographers, filmmakers, models, designers, athletes and dancers.


My Role: Manage social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest) including:
• Communicating with the Beazy community online.
• Instagram Photography competition and stories.
• Design (Canva/Preview) and scheduling (Buffer).
• Photo credits.
/!\ The pictures below were not taken by me. I just selected and scheduled them with descriptions for Beazy social media accounts. All photo credits can be found on their Instagram.





Basic strategy:
• Post at least once a day on each social media platform.

• Instagram: Visually-focused, beautiful photographs, “clean” professional look, with diversity and information. Focus on visual storytelling!.

• Facebook: Main platform to share textual content, blog posts, events but also re-post YouTube videos and podcasts.

• Pinterest: we started from scratch.

• Create more original content (e.g. talents profiles, #MondayMotivation, Tips for filmmakers and photographers, specific gear information, #ThrowbackThursdays, Podcast, etc.).
