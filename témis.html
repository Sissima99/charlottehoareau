TV Témis

January 2022 – May 2022 by Charlotte

I worked as a journalist for TV Témis, the community television of the regional county municipality of Temiscamingue in Quebec, Canada.


To follow them on Facebook, click here .


From January 2022, I was responsible for producing episodes for the local news show “Info-Témis” every week.
I had to:

• Research relevant topics.
• Cover events and go to press conferences.
• Interview local public figures.
• Write news reports and read them in front of the camera.
• (Sometimes) Host shows related to local news.

All episodes are available on the website and YouTube channel.
