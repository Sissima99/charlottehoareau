Who am I?

I come from beautiful Reunion Island, but I believe that home is not a place… it’s a feeling. I lived in South Africa, Germany, and I am currently exploring Canada. Where to next? Only time will tell.

I worked as a bilingual journalist and writer for various companies for over three years. Now, I consider myself a freelancer and digital nomad. I am passionate about storytelling, languages, and content creation.

I studied at the University of Cape Town, South Africa (Master’s in Media Studies and Communication) and at the University of British Columbia, Canada (Semester Exchange with Scholarship).

In addition to my love for writing, I love painting, spicy food, and plants. I can play the guitar, and I travel extensively (+20 countries).
