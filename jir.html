JIR GROUPE MEDIA

April 2021 – November 2021 by Charlotte

I worked as a journalist for the JIR MEDIA GROUP.


Launched in 1951, the JIR MEDIA GROUP is a press company specialising in local and national news in Reunion island. In association with the Cadjee Group since April 2009 (51 to 200 employees), JIR is one of the oldest newspapers on the island. In 1996, it was one of the first newspapers in France to launch a digital version:Clicanoo.re. Today the website is visited by more than 500 000 internet users every month.


The JIR MEDIA GROUP publishes:
• The Journal of Reunion Island (JIR) : daily newspaper (20 000 copies).

• Femme Magazine: monthly magazine for women ; available with the JIR with an audience of 80 000 readers per issue (25 000 copies).

• TV JIR: Weekly TV magazine supplement appearing on Saturdays (25 000 copies).

• Case Magazine: Quarterly Interior design and real estate magazine with nearly 80 000 readers per issue (25 000 copies).

• Made In Réunion: Bimonthly economic magazine (25 000 copies).

My Role:
• Write articles for the newspapers and the website.

• Contact, meet and interview stakeholders.

• Find relevant HD photographs.
